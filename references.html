References

A few companies we have worked with:

  • METEO France - www.meteo.fr
    METEO France is the french meteorology agency and the operator of an extensive network of ground, marine, airborne and space borne sensors. We have helped them move toward using interoperable technologies to prepare for new European regulations as well as reduce integration costs of new equipments.
  • The University of Alabama in Huntsville - www.uah.edu
    The University of Alabama in Huntsville (UAH) is well known for its research work with the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) and the US Department of Defense. Alexandre Robin, our President, worked there for 3 years as a Research Scientist in the Visualization and Systems Team (VAST) under the direction of Dr. Michael E. Botts, creator of the SensorML language.
  • Botts Innovative Research, Inc. - www.botts-inc.net
    Botts Innovative Research, Inc (Botts-Inc) specializes in the design and application of open standards for sensor systems within the intelligence, defense, and scientific communities.
  • VisioTerra - www.visioterra.fr
    A Scientific Consulting Company specialized in Earth Observation and its applications. Mainly working for ESA and other European space agencies.
  • SpotImage - www.spotimage.fr
    A French company located in the suburb of Toulouse commercializing earth imagery acquired by the French satellite SPOT and others. This company contributes to OGC and is a great supporter of open standards that can improve interoperability between imagery vendors and their customers.
  • Seicorp - www.seicorp.com
    SeiCorp, Inc. is a woman owned, small business corporation focusing on government intelligence processes, providing critical expertise in determining and defining intelligence requirements, and bringing them to Agency and military service users in an ever changing Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR) environment.
  • Magellium - www.magellium.fr
    Magellium is a company of specialists on the leading edge of technology who leverage their expert knowledge in signal and image processing, geomatics, innovative learning technologies, robotics and intelligent systems to offer high value-added engineering, systems design, solutions and services.
