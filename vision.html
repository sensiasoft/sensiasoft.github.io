Our Vision

We at Sensia Software believe strongly that Sustainable Development is a key to a more balanced, and peaceful future. Whether we work with small businesses, bigger corporations, humanitarian organizations or developing nations, our goal is to use innovation and technology to reach this goal faster. As an applied research company, we feel that our mission is to share our knowledge and experience to improve the quality of life around the world on an everyday basis. We do this mainly by developing efficient and interoperable IT solutions that can dramatically improve human and natural resources management in extreme situations such as natural disasters or military aggression.

We are confident that our work can benefit various communities by making processes more efficient, systems easier to manage and information more accessible across the planet. We understand that most of our customers' goal is not technology in itself so, even though we are very knowledgeable about software development and information technology in general, we always try to understand the heart of the problem first. We see smart IT technologies as useful tools to achieve more important strategic goals such as, for instance, increasing efficiency and interoperability or reducing response time in emergency situations. This is why we pay a lot of attention to the real needs behind the IT infrastructure and try to understand the technical and/or human consequences of our work.
